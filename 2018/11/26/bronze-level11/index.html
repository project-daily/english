🗝 put

BrE /pʊt/
NAmE /pʊt/

    • to move sth to an earlier time or date 将…提前

        我们已把婚礼提前了一周。

  • put sb off sth/sb

    • to make sb lose interest in or enthusiasm for sth/sb 使失去兴趣（或热情）

        老师教得不好使他失去了对理科的兴趣。

  • put sth↔off

    • to change sth to a later time or date 推迟；延迟
      • We’ve had to put off our wedding until September.
        我们只得把婚期推迟到九月。
